Understand the 4 types of cash flow
Problem-Solving Assistant.html

1. Lump Sums (CH3)

Main features: One-time money deposited/withdrawn in a year

2. Uneven Multiple Cash Flow

Main features: Unequal amounts of money deposited/withdrawn in multiple years

Key words: You deposit $xxx next year, $yyy the year after that, and then $zzz three years out.

3. Perpetuities and Delayed Perpetuity

Main features: An equal periodic cash flow stream that will never cease.

Key words in question: pays (you) $xxx per year forever

4. (Ordinary) Annuities

Main features: Equal amounts deposited/withdrawn over multiple years

Key words: deposit $xxx at the end of each of the next N years
